Anna Akana sends a message to viewers who might be thinking about committing suicide, discussing the repercussions amongst people close to them, using her own personal experience with her sister who committed suicide in 2007

Jessica Gimeno: How to get stuff done when you are depressed

Dr. Eliza Noh shares her story of growing up in Texas, self-discovery in New York, and contributing to the ethnic & women's studies movement in California.

Model Minority: Do The Math

Reveals the impact of the model minority myth on the experiences and perspectives of Asian American college students. The myth is a complex and contradictory stereotype of Asian Americans as academic over-achievers. While many believe the stereotype is positive, it causes many problems.
