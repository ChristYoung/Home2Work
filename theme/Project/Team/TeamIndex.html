WHAT WE DO?

Help transform how Asian women and their loved ones perceive mental health and receive mental health services!

Together Empowering Asian Minds(TEAM) and our supportors understand the stigma of depression, mental health, and the it.
We collaborated with two awesome women in the AAPI community to get the world out about mental health awareness: Flamics and Hapa Time.
TEAM campaign is generously funded by HOPE & Grace Initiative.
